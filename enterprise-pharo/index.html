Enterprise Pharo

Pharo is a clean, innovative, open-source, live-programming environment.

Enterprise Pharo is the third volume of a series of book covering Pharo and its community. Whereas the first volume is intended for newcomers and the second volume covers deep topics of Pharo, this third volume covers libraries and frameworks for enterprises, and in particular those doing web development.
